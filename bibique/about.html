Welcome to BIBIQUE STORY!







Our Story

BIBIQUE SHOP was established back in the 1990s. The shop was originally started by our grandmother, passed down to my mother, and now it has been passed down to me. Our secret recipe has been kept and cherished through generations, ensuring the same great taste that our customers have loved for decades.










Family Tradition

Barangay 267, also known as Barangay Alvarez, has been served by our BBQ for almost 40 years and still counting. The community loves our BBQ so much that it has become a staple in their daily lives. We take pride in our family tradition and the love we put into every bite.










Our Expansion

Today, BIBIQUE SHOP has grown to 22 branches around the Philippines. Our commitment to quality and tradition has allowed us to expand and share our delicious BBQ with more people. We are grateful for the support of our loyal customers and look forward to serving you for many more years to come.
